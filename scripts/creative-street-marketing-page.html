What is street furniture advertising?


Street furniture advertising is any type of advertising that is placed on public buildings. It can be posters or billboards. It's an important and effective marketing strategy for new businesses. It helps them reach specific groups of people or areas. Street advertising is cheaper than large-format billboards.

Street marketing is a type of marketing that uses the environment around a product or service to reach target customers. This marketing usually happens on streets, sidewalks, and transit stations.

Brands use street marketing and advertising to create a sense of community around a product or service. They often target end users who are unlikely to be reached through traditional and digital marketing channels such as print or television advertising. Street marketing brings products or services directly to customers in a public place.

Street marketing and advertising have many benefits. It lets brands connect with their target customers. It also makes customers loyal and excited about new products or services. Street marketing is affordable because it doesn't require large advertising campaigns.

Common types of street marketing and advertising


Ads on street furniture come in many different formats. These formats use public places, street placements, and fixtures to deliver messages directly to people walking by and people driving.

  • Bus Shelter Advertising: Bus shelters let advertisers reach two types of people at once: pedestrians waiting for public transportation and passengers in passing vehicles. Advertisers can take advantage of this by putting ads on both the inside and outside of the shelter.
  • Kiosks: Kiosks are like small stores. They can show posters, screens, or other things that you can see to tell people about businesses, events, or public services. Kiosks can also be used for small-scale retail, offering food, drinks, souvenirs, or other goods.
  • Newsstand Ads: Ads on newsstands are placed in a way that makes them easy for the people you want to reach to see. People often read the news while waiting for the bus or train.
  • Digital Street Furniture Ads: The way people advertise on street corners is changing to digital. Static ads are being replaced with dynamic digital screens that use visuals to attract viewers and offer interactive experiences. This technological change is a reason why street furniture advertising is still so popular.

Level Up Your OOH Strategy with Street Furniture Ads


Advertising on street furniture can be a very powerful tool for your out-of-home (OOH) marketing strategy. When you place your ad in the right spot, it puts your brand right in front of people walking by. This is a special chance to get their attention during their everyday lives.

Here is how street furniture advertising can help your outdoor advertising plan:

  • Who we're trying to reach: Focus on specific types of people in busy cities.
  • Engagement: Use creative messaging in places where people walk.
  • It's also cost-effective. It's a budget-friendly way to amplify your outdoor advertising campaign.
  • Local Focus: It's great for getting the attention of residents and businesses.

Incorporating street advertising into your outdoor advertising strategy can create a more comprehensive and impactful campaign.
