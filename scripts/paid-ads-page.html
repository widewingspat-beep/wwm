PPC for Key Dubai Industries


Industries in Dubai use PPC in different ways.

Best Ad Formats for Real Estate:


Connect with wealthy individuals who want luxury properties in Marina, Palm Jumeirah, or Business Bay.

  • Search ads (Google Ads) showing high-intent keywords will show results. For example: “Luxury apartments Dubai Marina,” “Villas for sale Palm Jumeirah,” and “Investment property Dubai.”
  • Display or remarketing ads can be shown to users who have viewed property pages or listings. Use Dynamic Search Ads (DSA) or Responsive Search Ads to reach more users.
  • YouTube video ads showing virtual tours and explaining its lifestyle in high-end areas.

Targeting Examples for Real Estate

  • Demographics: Wealthy expats and investors are interested in Dubai. They are between the ages of 30 and 60. These investors come from the UK, India, China, and the GCC.
  • Geographic targeting: Specific Emirates or areas (Dubai Marina, Palm Jumeirah, Business Bay).
  • Interest/affinity: Property investors, luxurious lifestyles, global relocation.
  • Device targeting: Use a desktop for research and use mobile phones when one needs a quick view or forms that can be completed.

Seasonal Calendar

  • October-December: From October to December, engage in real estate fairs. Build up a marketing plan for this important selling time prior to New Year. It is the time buyers will normally set up their budgets and search more, in anticipation of more interest.
  • January-March: After the Dubai Shopping Festival, many wealthy visitors stay and look for properties. This is also a great time for promoting investments.
  • Summer (June-August): Slower market, but opportunity for off-plan promotions or virtual campaigns for overseas buyers.
  • Expo- or event-based spikes: when international events bring foreign visitors (e.g., trade expos, conference seasons).

Case study: Luxury apartments in Dubai Marina

Challenge:


A developer wanted to sell 25 luxury apartments in Dubai Marina in four months. They had a small marketing budget.

Action:


A Google Search & Display remarketing campaign was launched, targeting UK, Indian, and GCC nationals interested in property investment. This included a YouTube video tour of the property. An optimized landing page with a strong call to action was also used. Responsive search ads were part of the strategy too.

Results:

  • 35% of clicks came from remarketing
  • Cost per acquisition fell by 40% over the first month
  • 60% of the units were sold by the end of the campaign
  • ROI was ~4x ad spend

Best Ad Formats for Hospitality & Tourism


Advertise staycation deals, travel packages, and event experiences to GCC travellers and international visitors.

  • Search ads for travel-related terms such as “staycation Dubai,” “luxury resorts UAE,” and “hotel deals in Dubai.”
  • Show ads and display retargeting to past visitors or lookers.
  • Social media carousel ads & Reels (Instagram, TikTok) showing experience, atmosphere, food info, and events.
  • Video ads (YouTube, Meta) with virtual tours and guest testimonials.
  • Google Hotel Ads / Travel Ads if relevant.

Targeting Examples for Hospitality & Tourism

  • Demographics: GCC nationals, expats, and international tourists (depending on the time of year) looking for travel, luxury, and experiences.
  • Geographic targeting: in feeder markets (India, UK, Saudi Arabia, Russia) and also targeting domestic travel (Dubai / neighboring emirates).
  • Interest targeting: hotel stays, luxury travel, wellness, food tourism, and event-goers.
  • Behavioral remarketing: people who visited a hotel or destination site, looked at rooms, and left without booking.

Seasonal Calendar

  • Winter (November-March): peak travel season, highest demand.
  • Ramadhan & Eid periods: Greater activity in local and regional travel; special offers for iftar and staycation.
  • Summer (June-August): domestic travel, "Beat the heat" offers, spa packages.
  • Event/Holiday tie-ins: New Year’s Eve, Dubai Shopping Festival, cultural festivals.

Case Study: Staycation Packages for GCC Markets


Problem: A boutique hotel in Dubai wanted to fill its rooms during the summer. This time is usually slow mid-week, with occupancy below 40%.

Action:


I created Instagram Reels and Facebook Carousel ads for GCC nationals. These ads promoted packages called Mid-week Stay and Spa. The packages included dining allowances.

I also retargeted those who had viewed hotel pages or Instagram posts. In addition to that, I launched Google Search campaigns for Dubai hotel staycation deals.

Result: 70% increase in midweek bookings compared to baseline. Bookings per cost were reduced by 30% for the summer campaign of the previous year. Social engagement also increased, with user-generated content posts up by 45%.

Ad Formats—Best for Retail and E-Commerce


Increase sales performance during busy times, e.g., Dubai Shopping Festival and during Ramadan. Use results-driven advertising to help.

  • Google shopping ads for product visibility.
  • Search ads for long-tail search terms, e.g., designer handbags sale Dubai, online electronics store UAE.
  • Dynamic display ads and retarget cart abandoners.
  • Social Commerce Ads (in Instagram Shop, Facebook Shop, and TikTok)
  • Flash sale or timed promo ads with a countdown.

Targeting Examples for Retail & E-Commerce

  • Demographic: Younger ages (18-35). Fashion and gadgets interest expatriates and locals.
  • Geographic: Dubai and neighboring Emirates (Sharjah, Abu Dhabi), and also the GCC cross-border if a shipping facility exists.
  • Seasonal Shoppers: Have previously shopped during off-sales and are newsletter subscribers.
  • Behavioral: Cart abandoners viewed particular categories of products.

Seasonal Calendar

  • Dubai Shopping Festival (December-January): big retail surge, huge sales.
  • Ramadan & Eid promotions: great opportunities for special offers & discounts.
  • Back to school (August-September): electronics, school supplies, kids’ clothing.
  • Summer sales & mid-year clearance: slower months but good for clearance promotions and fashion lines.

Case Study: E-commerce Electronics Store

Challenge:


An online store selling consumer electronics saw high add-to-cart rates but low checkout completion.

Action:


We launched dynamic remarketing ads on Google Display and Facebook for people who left items in their carts. We also ran "flash sale" search ads during back-to-school season. We used Shopping Ads with clear promotional messages. Finally, we improved the mobile checkout process.

Results:


Cart abandonment fell by 25%. Conversions rose by 50% during the promotion. The return on ad spend (ROAS) was about 6 times for the flash sale.

Most PPC campaigns can show results in a couple of weeks. This, of course, depends on the budget, level of competition, and the optimization of the campaign.

Is PPC suitable for small businesses in Dubai?
