What is cinema advertising?


This type of advertising is called “cinema advertising.” It uses different technologies. Cinemas use different areas to show different types of advertising, such as static posters, digital ads, and videos.

Cinema advertising comes in many forms.


  • Video Ads: Before the movie trailers start, you may see video ads or commercials for the cinema. These are usually similar to the kinds of commercials you see on TV.
  • Slideshow ads: These are the ads that play on the screen as you enter the theater and choose your seat. These are called “silent, still advertisements” because they’re so quiet and you can’t see them.
  • Kiosk ads: Ads on cinema kiosks are called “kiosk ads.” These ads are usually placed in the lobby and can be digital or static.
  • Standees: These are large, stand-alone ads that look like giant posters.
  • LED screens: When people go to the cinema, they see more than just the screen. Many theaters offer digital advertising on smaller LED screens throughout the building.

Common Questions About Cinema Advertising


What is cinema advertising?

Cinema ads are short advertisements that play before trailers and films start. They play as people enter the theater, find their seats, and get comfortable. Ads like this are called “out-of-home advertising” because they reach people when they’re outside.

Ads can be shown on the big screen in the theater, in the lobby, on posters, or even on popcorn bags. One of the unique advantages of cinema advertising is that it offers a captive audience.

What is cinema marketing?

Cinema advertising is when ads appear on screens in multiplex cinemas and single-screen theaters. Cinema advertising includes ads on slideshows and video ads. Cinema advertising also uses other media, such as kiosk advertising, standees (large boards), seatback covers (advertising on the back of a chair), and advertising on LED screens.

What are the advantages of using cinema advertising?

Ads on screens have a bigger impact on their audience because they are so big. This makes them more interesting to watch than other types of advertising. Cinema advertising uses both sound and sight, so audiences easily understand the brand messages. Also, it costs much less than if the same number of people were exposed to TV or internet advertising.

How often do movies show ads in theaters?

There are many ways to advertise your brand in cinemas. You can choose to advertise in movie theaters. This includes video and slide ads, as well as static ads on film promos. How often you see these ads depends on the package you choose, or, in other words, how much you pay for them.

For example, you can put your brand name in the ad once or even five times. Each screen in the movie theater shows four movies a day, for a total of 28 screenings per week. Ads like this allow you to send your brand messages to a lot of people.

What’s the cost to advertise on the big screen in theaters, both in small, independent theaters and in multiplexes with lots of screens?

The cost to advertise in movie theaters depends on a few things: the city, the time of day, how many seats are available, the popularity of the theater, and the type of audience that visits.

Are the rates for in-cinema advertising different depending on which films are shown?

Most people want to see blockbuster movies, and there is a higher demand for them than for other movies. This means that companies that want you to buy their products pay more for ads during these movies. So, you might have to pay more for ads during popular movies. The cost of your ad could be two or three times the usual price for a branded ad during regular showings.

Are there any time limits for advertising in movie theaters?

Advertising for movies in movie theaters must start on a Friday. That’s the day when new films are released in theaters with only one screen or theaters with many screens (called multiplexes). Ads, including video ads and slides, are automatically updated when new entries are made.

However, you must finish paying for your ad campaign and deliver your ad with the brand to the cinema screens by 5 pm on Wednesday. If you do this, it will start playing the following Friday.
