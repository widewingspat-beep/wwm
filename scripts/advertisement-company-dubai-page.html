Advertisements in the United Arab Emirates (UAE)


Are you thinking about advertising in the UAE? If you're handling it in-house or outsourcing it to experts, it's important to understand the rules. The government has some requirements, but a good advertisement company in Dubai can help make it easier.

New Rules for Ads


There are some new advertising standards in the UAE. They aim to ensure that advertisements are respectful, truthful, and fair, while also helping the country's economy. Here's what you need to know:

  • Respectful Ads: They need to respect local culture, religion, and traditions. And they shouldn't say anything bad about the government or its symbols.
  • No-No Products: You can't advertise alcohol, tobacco, drugs, or other items that are banned.
  • Keep it clean. Was it offensive language or images? No. Ads can't encourage violence or hate, either.
  • Privacy Matters: Don't spread any rumours or fake news that could hurt someone's privacy.
  • Protecting Consumers: Ads have to follow the rules to protect consumers and ensure fair competition.
  • Health Ads: If you're promoting medicine, it has to meet health regulations.

Advertising Content Requirements


Now, about the content. Ads should be in Arabic or the local language. And they have to follow these rules:

  • Clear and True: No tricks are allowed. Ads should be easy to understand and truthful.
  • Respect Trademarks: Don't use someone else's brand without permission. That's not allowed.
  • Get Approval: Some ads, like those for medicine or education, need to be approved by the right authority before they can be shared.

If you want to advertise in the UAE, you need to know the rules. These rules are in place to ensure fairness for everyone.
